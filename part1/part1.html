DRAG CIRCLE GENERATOR

BLUE CIRCLE GENERATION:
Click on the grid and drag the mouse.When you releases the mouse button,the program should
highlight the points that correspond to the edge of the circle,in such a way that there is a
continuous single line of points which closely approximate the edge of the circle.


RED CIRCLE GENERATION:
Two additional circles should be created corresponding to the largest and smallest radius of the
highlighted points such that the area between the two red circles is minimized but also enclose
all parts of all highlighted points.
